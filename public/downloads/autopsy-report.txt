CITY MORGUE — OFFICE OF THE MEDICAL EXAMINER
CONFIDENTIAL AUTOPSY REPORT
================================================

CASE No.: NR-1948-0031
DECEDENT: Vivian Lockhart, F, 34
DATE OF EXAMINATION: November 1, 1948

CAUSE OF DEATH: Asphyxiation.
MANNER OF DEATH: Homicide.

FINDINGS
--------
- Petechial hemorrhaging consistent with manual strangulation.
- Faint bruising to the left wrist; defensive in nature.
- Trace residue of a distinctive amber cologne on the collar.
- Time of death estimated between 10:45 PM and 11:30 PM.

NOTES
-----
The absence of forced entry suggests the victim knew her assailant.
A single black glove fiber was recovered beneath the fingernails.

Examiner: Dr. H. Merrick
